Rules

  • The contest begins at 2:30 and ends at 3:00.
  • No hacking the submission system.
  • Participants must register on the main page in order to participate, and must be logged in to submit problems. You may register at any time, including after the contest has begun.
  • The contest consists of eight problems of equal weight.
  • Scoring is based off of the number of succesful hacks, the time of each succesful hack, and the number of failed hacks. For a single problem, the score starts at one point and dimishes two hudredths of a point per minute. An additional tenth of a point is deducted for each failed submission. However, the minimum score for a single problem is three tenths of a point, regardless of time or submisssion count. Therefore, the final score for a participant is $\sum \textrm{max} \left( 1 - .02 \times \textrm{minutes after start} - .1 \times \textrm{failed submissions}, .3 \right)$ for each solved problem.
